What is Icare ?

Icare webapp is a health care network tool that targets Doctors,Nurses,lab and patients family members.

How it works ?

It works by connecting all health care members in one place so they can manage their patients efficiently.

Description:

It gives you the ability to create new patient accounts as an admin and add,link other member accounts like nurses and doctors to help manage the patient case quickly and in a more accurate way.In addition,you can create a family portal account which helps family monitor their patients case from phone.

Features

  • Create New Patient Account
  • Create New Sub Nurses Account
  • Create New Family Account
  • Link Doctors Accounts To Patient Account
  • Manage Patient Vitals,Medications,Procedures
  • Share Record With Doctors
  • Doctors By Their Own Account can order new medications and view patient status
  • Admin can Scheduale team members shifting
